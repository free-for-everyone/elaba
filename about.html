About

e-laba

The “e-laba” is an e-commerce platform for laundry shop that empowers them with a web application that will help them connect with their customer, then there will be a mobile application provided to local riders with the opportunity to deliver the laundry, and lastly the user can choose the laundry shop they wanted their clothes to be washed on the tips of their finger.

The mobile app include kilograms of clothes and amount of each services. We will also provide a web platform for the laundry shop to monitor the request. The rider will have also be in the mobile application and will be immediately notify about the customer’s request. The e-laba will provide the tools necessary such as laundry bags, for pick up and delivery. And lastly, transaction can be done through cash on hand and digital payment.
